MaxLoad Desktop

The same automation engine — now a standalone app, no Chrome extension.

  1. Type your Maximo URL in the address bar above and press Enter.
  2. Log in normally — password, SSO, or MFA. MaxLoad never sees your credentials; you sign in to Maximo directly, just like in a browser.
  3. Once Maximo loads, the MaxLoad panel on the right activates. Teach, bind, and run exactly as before.
Your login session is remembered between runs (until Maximo's own timeout), so you normally sign in once. Toggle the panel with the Panel button.
